THe PROBLEM

THE SUPREME COURT RULING in June 2021 Allowing Amateur Athletes to be paid on their on Name, Image and Likeness (NIL) has changed college athletics forever. This Decision generated a Law Suit Against The NCAA for damages back to 2016 for athletes not paid. The NCaa Case is In the Final Process of Being Settled $2.8B, forcing the major universities to pay up to $20M per year for the next 10 years.
